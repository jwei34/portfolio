FUTI: An On-Demand Delivery Startup

Timeline: June-Aug 2020; 10 weeks | Role: Web & Graphic Design Intern | Team: Marketing

Introduction •  App Store Screenshots •  Icons •  Landing Page •  Marketing Material

Introduction

In the summer of 2020, I worked as a Design Intern for FUTI, an early-stage startup based in Budapest, Hungary. FUTI was an on-demand delivery platform that delivered anything from food and groceries to any retail products available in the city. My contributions included creating app icons (Adoble Illustrator), marketing material for Facebook and Instagram (AI, PS), and the landing page (Adobe XD, Elementor).

Icons for the App

I made a range of icons using Adobe Ps for the Futi mobile application. These are examples of some that were used to browse product categories. My goal was to create icons that not only visually engaged users but also made the represented product categories instantly clear.

Landing Page Iterations

In the pre-launch version of the landing page, I focused on communicating the key features of FUTI: what it is (a mobile app), what is does (on-demand delivery), how it works (3 easy steps), what is sells (everything!), and how one can join (either as a vendor or courier).

In the post-launch version of landing page, I aimed to bring more life & color to the interface by incorporating a hero I created using Adobe Illustrator, as well as more vibrant background colors.
